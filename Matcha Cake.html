Matcha Cake

Ingredients

The Crust
-Oreo 10 pieces
-Butter 40 g

The Filling
-Cream cheese 200 g
-Granulated sugar 100 g
-Sour cream 90g
-Fresh cream 200 g
-3 eggs
-Cornstarch 2 tablespoons
-Matcha powder 10 g

Recipe

1. Place the oreo in a bag and crush it.
2. Add butter and mix them well, put it in a mold and cool in a refrigerator for about 30 minutes.
3. Put all "The Filling" ingredients except the matcha powder in a mixer and crush until smooth.
4. Mix the matcha powder in half of the filling.
5. Fill up the mold with the white filling and matcha filing alternately .
6. Put it into the oven and set it to 160 Â° C for 60 minutes.
7. After baking, let it cool, as it is cooled down, put it in the refrigerator overnight.
8. Ready to eat.
